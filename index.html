About Me

Passionate researcher exploring the intersection of AI and cybersecurity

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas, where I conduct cutting-edge research in Large Language Models (LLMs) and their applications in cybersecurity. My work focuses on developing innovative Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) approaches for cyber attack detection in critical infrastructure systems.

With a strong foundation in machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, I have published research at prestigious IEEE and ACM conferences. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Military Institute of Science and Technology in Bangladesh, where I graduated with a 3.88 GPA.

Beyond research, I am passionate about teaching and have served as both a Graduate Teaching Assistant and Lecturer, helping over 400 students master complex concepts in computer science, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.

Research Projects

Innovative solutions at the intersection of AI and real-world challenges

Current

Multimodal RAG for Cyber Attack Detection in Transit Systems

Developed an innovative integration of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with LLMs for transit system security, achieving 42.5% precision—a 73.65% improvement over baseline—in detecting cyber threats using updated MITRE ATT&CK datasets.

RAG Cybersecurity LLMs

Survey on Multimodal Large Language Models

Conducted an in-depth analysis of 14 fundamental MLLM papers and their derivatives, providing a detailed comparison of techniques, applications, and advancements in the field.

MLLMs Survey Multimodal AI

Dynamic Hand Gesture Recognition for ASL

Designed a novel 3D skeletal model and depth imaging approach, achieving 98.6% accuracy in recognizing dynamic gestures using spatiotemporal analysis of joint angles.

Computer Vision ASL Deep Learning

Sea-Level Prediction Using ML/DL

Conducted a study on tidal height forecasting in Bangladesh's coastal regions using Machine Learning and Deep Learning, achieving up to 0.94 R² accuracy with Neural Networks.

Climate Science Prediction Neural Networks

Voice-Controlled Intelligent Robotic Arm

Developed an AI-based voice-controlled robotic arm system to assist surgeons in performing surgery, integrating natural language processing with robotic control systems.

Robotics Voice Control Healthcare

AI-Based Temperature Sensing System

Created Thermique, an integrated AI-based temperature sensing and management system designed to help contain COVID-19 contamination through intelligent monitoring.

IoT Healthcare AI
